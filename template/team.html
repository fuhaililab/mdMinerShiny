Three Sigma Medicine

Led by Dr. Fuhai Li, Three Sigma is a bioinformatic lab consisting of a group of bright professors, researchers and students from various background such as computing, chemistry and biology. We aim at developing reliable approach to provide precision medicine for cancer patienets.

Dr.Fuhai Li

Fuhai Li, PhD, is an assistant professor in the Department of Biomedical Informatics. His primary research interests are to apply mathematical, statistical learning, and machine learning approaches on big and diverse data sets in order to solve biological and clinical challenges in treating complex diseases, (e.g. cancers).

Haoyang Wu

Haoyang is a junior undergraduate student at The Ohio State University. He studies computer science & engineering with a minor in economics. He is interested in studying various computing techniques and their various application in real world. With a strong interest in healthcare, Haoyang hopes what he learns from and contributes to Three Sigma Medicine can make a difference to patients somewhere in the world.
